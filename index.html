About me

I am a PhD student in the Binsec group at CEA List working under the supervision of Sebastien Bardin, Matthieu Lemerre, and Jean-Yves Marion.

I am interested in software safety and security and the application of formal methods and machine learning in the context of binary-level security analyses.

Previously, I received a master's degree in computer science in 2017 from Chennai Mathematical Institute. Here is my resume.

Master's project
